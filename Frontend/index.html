About Us

Our institution is made by a group of girls who are very concerned in problems in their community and who have taken the will to solve it. We are a non-profit organization built up to stop gender based violence(GBV). Having witnessed many lives being ruined because of 1 moment in their life, we coudn't keep silent and watch while it keeps happening.

Our Mission

Setting it apart from any type of violence, GBV puts a life long scar in the victims mind that prohibits them from effectivey working, learn or being productive, leading to many personal, economical and psychological crisis.Therefore, our mission is to stop Gender-Based-Violence.

Our Plan

To help everyone who has been violated, who is being violated and who might be prone to violation. Further expand our coverage to different regions and collaborate with partners to make a lasting impact.

Our Vision

To see a world clear of traumatized victims of GBV by working together as one with every part of the community.
